Privacy Policy
How we handle information
This site is operated by Adex Holdings Trust. For questions, contact tenantservices@adexholdings.com or 949-415-4633.
Information you provide
  • Tenant Portal submissions: When you submit a request, you provide details such as name, email, phone, property selection, and message content. These are sent to us via Formspree and used only to respond to your request.
Automatic information (optional security logging)
  • Basic visit logs: If enabled, the site sends the page path, title, and referrer to a security logging endpoint.
  • IP address: If enabled, the security logging endpoint may record the IP address as seen by its provider (for example, Cloudflare) along with user agent and timestamp for fraud and abuse prevention.
  • Purpose: Security monitoring, abuse prevention, and operational troubleshooting.
  • Retention: We recommend retaining logs for a limited period (for example, 30–90 days) unless needed for security investigations.
Cookies and local storage
  • This site may use local storage to remember rental availability when the Cloudflare Worker is not configured. This is stored only in your browser.
  • No third-party advertising cookies are used by default.
CAPTCHA / spam protection
  • We recommend enabling Cloudflare Turnstile on the Tenant Portal form to reduce spam.
  • Turnstile may collect device/browser signals to detect automated traffic.
Your choices
  • You may request deletion of Tenant Portal submissions by contacting us.
  • If you do not want automatic security logging, the site owner can disable it by removing the security endpoint configuration.
Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page.
